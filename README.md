## About Me

Before "automation" became a buzzword on every SaaS landing page, I was already building it — by hand, in code.

For the past 8+ years I've designed and shipped the systems that sit *behind* a business: the OCR pipeline that reads a document so a human doesn't have to, the pricing engine that quotes a job in seconds instead of hours, the CRM that knows what a client needs before the sales team asks. My work lives at the intersection of **backend engineering, infrastructure, and applied AI** — built to remove manual work permanently, not just speed it up temporarily.

I think in systems, not tickets. Give me a broken or absent process, and I'll architect, build, and own it end-to-end.

## Philosophy

> I don't automate tasks. I automate business processes.

A task is one click. A process is the chain of decisions, handoffs, and exceptions behind it. I build for the second — because that's where the real cost of manual work lives, and that's where automation actually pays off.
